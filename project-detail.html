E-Commerce Redesign 2023

FASHION RETAIL UX OVERHAUL

Complete transformation of shopping experience for a leading fashion retailer, increasing conversions by 35% and reducing cart abandonment by 28%.

35% Increase in Conversions
28% Reduction in Cart Abandonment
4.8★ App Store Rating

The Challenge

The client was experiencing high bounce rates (68%) and low conversion (1.2%) on their mobile e-commerce platform, despite mobile accounting for 72% of their traffic.

My Role

Lead UI/UX Designer responsible for research, wireframing, prototyping, and overseeing front-end implementation.

Duration

6 months (Research to Implementation)

Tools Used

Figma Adobe XD Hotjar Google Analytics

IDENTIFYING THE PROBLEMS

01

Confusing Navigation

Users struggled to find products, with 42% dropping off at category pages.

02

Slow Performance

3.8s average load time led to 53% of users leaving before page load.

03

Outdated Checkout

7-step process with 68% abandonment rate at payment stage.

Research Methods

User Interviews
Analytics Review
Clickstream Analysis
Heatmaps

DESIGN SOLUTIONS

01. Streamlined Navigation

Implemented mega-menu with visual categories and predictive search reduced clicks to product by 62%.

+42% Category Engagement -62% Clicks to Product

02. Performance Optimization

Redesigned asset loading strategy decreased load time to 1.2s (68% improvement).

1.2s Load Time -68% Bounce Rate

03. One-Page Checkout

Consolidated 7-step process into single-page with progress indicators reduced abandonment by 28%.

+28% Completion 4.8★ User Rating
